## The Journey: From Dashboards to Data Science

### The Visualization Spark
My professional journey began in the world of observability. Working with tools like **ELK (Elasticsearch, Logstash, Kibana)**, **Grafana**, and **Splunk**, I helped clients build dashboards to make sense of their system data.  

Driven by a desire to tell better stories, I dove into **data visualization** to learn how to extract and present actionable insights to stakeholders.

### The Engineering Pivot
Once I understood the power of the **output**, I became fascinated by the **input**.

I transitioned into **Data Engineering**, where I discovered a passion for building automated **ETL workflows**. There is a unique satisfaction in replacing repetitive manual tasks with a completed automated pipeline that saves time and scales efficiently.

### The Web3 Challenge & The "Aha!" Moment
At **BTSE Exchange**, I had the opportunity to work in the fast-paced **Web3 domain**. My role was a hybrid of **Data Analyst, Data Engineer, and Data Scientist**, allowing me to build workflows and models with significant creative freedom.

However, collaborating with senior Data Scientists revealed the depth of theoretical modeling I still wanted to master. This realization led me to **Chicago** to pursue my **Master of Science in Data Science at the Illinois Institute of Technology (IIT).**
